Bringing African Stories

KingZToonZ is a premier film and animation studio dedicated to empowering the next generation of African filmmakers, animators, and storytellers through world-class production and community funding.

View Our Work Support The Mission

Empowering Voices,
Animating Futures

Founded with a vision to decentralize global animation and film production, KingZToonZ provides resources, mentorship, and funding to talented creators across the African continent.

We believe that Africa's rich heritage, diverse cultures, and untold stories deserve to be seen on the world's biggest screens. By bridging the gap between raw talent and industry-standard tools, we are building a sustainable ecosystem for African creative arts.

  • $100 funds a high-quality drawing tablet for a rural creator.
